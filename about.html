About me

Helping children find their voice

Qualified, compassionate, and deeply committed to every child's wellbeing.

Dr. Kiran Deep Kaur B.A. Psychology (Hons), M.A. Psychology, Special B.Ed (Visual Impairment), PGDCA

Hello, I'm Dr. Kiran Deep Kaur

I am a licensed child and adolescent psychologist with over 15 years of experience working with children, young people, and their families. My practice is built on the belief that every child — regardless of their abilities or challenges — has the potential to flourish.

My journey into psychology began with a deep curiosity about how children develop, learn, and make sense of the world around them. Over the years, I have had the privilege of working with a wide range of children, including those with ADHD, intellectual disabilities, visual impairments, and other special needs.

"I believe the most powerful thing we can offer a struggling child is a space where they feel truly seen, heard, and safe."

I trained at Panjab University, Chandigarh, and have since worked in schools, hospitals, and community clinics. I work collaboratively with parents, teachers, and other professionals to create wraparound support that extends beyond the therapy room.

Outside of work, I love reading, spending time in nature, and being with family. I believe that warmth and human connection are at the heart of good therapy.

How I work

My therapeutic approach

No two children are the same. I draw on a range of evidence-based methods, adapting my approach to the individual child and family.

  • ✓

    Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)Helping children identify and change unhelpful thought patterns and behaviours in a structured, goal-oriented way.

  • ✓

    Play TherapyUsing play as a natural language for younger children to process emotions and experiences in a safe, expressive environment.

  • ✓

    Behavioural InterventionsPractical, structured strategies for managing challenging behaviours, particularly for children with ADHD and intellectual disabilities.

  • ✓

    Parent CoachingEquipping parents with understanding, skills, and confidence to support their child's growth between sessions.

  • ✓

    Psychoeducational AssessmentComprehensive assessments to identify a child's strengths, difficulties, and learning profile, with clear recommendations for home and school.
